Overview:

    

In this course, students will gain a foundational understanding of UI/UX design, learning how to create user-friendly interfaces that enhance the overall user experience. The course emphasizes practical skills using Figma, a powerful tool for designing, prototyping, and collaborating on digital projects. By the end, learners will be equipped to design intuitive, visually appealing interfaces with Figma, while applying core UI/UX principles.
